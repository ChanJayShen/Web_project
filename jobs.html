Company Description

Our Mission:

We are an indie game studio dedicated to creating innovative and engaging gaming experiences. We are looking for skilled and motivated individuals to join our team.

Why we need you:

We have available positions that involve designing, developing, and maintaining software applications, as well as collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality products.

Why you should join us:

If you are passionate about game development and eager to contribute to innovative projects, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity. Join our dynamic team and help us shape the future of indie games development!

Positions

      Junior UI/UX Designer:
    • Design the artistic elements and user interfaces for our game titles.
    • Reference No.: UI011
    • As a UI/UX Designer, you will be responsible for creating intuitive and engaging user interfaces for our game titles. You will work closely with development teams to ensure that our products are both visually appealing and easy to use.
    • Salary: $50,000 - $70,000 per year
    • Reporting line: Lead UI/UX Director
      Junior Engine Programmer:
    • A position working on the development and maintenance of our game engine for our applications.
    • Reference No.: JP020
    • As a Junior Engine Programmer, you will be responsible for working on crucial aspects of Engine Development, writing performant, robust, and maintainable code, and debugging, profiling, and optimizing code to meet quality and performance targets.
    • Salary: $60,000 - $80,000 per year
    • Reporting line: Lead Engine Programmer
      DevOps Engineer:
    • This position is responsible for managing the infrastructure and deployment processes of our games, ensuring efficient and reliable deployment.
    • Reference No.: DO130
    • As a DevOps Engineer, you will be responsible for managing the infrastructure and deployment processes of our game files. You will work closely with development teams to ensure that our applications are deployed efficiently and reliably.
    • Salary: $85,000 - $110,000 per year
    • Reporting line: Product Manager
