Company Description

Our Mission:

We are an indie game studio dedicated to creating innovative and engaging gaming experiences. We are looking for skilled and motivated individuals to join our team.

Why we need you:

We have available positions that involve designing, developing, and maintaining the fundamental aspects of our titles, as well as collaborating with diverse work-teams to deliver high-quality products.

Why you should join us:

If you are passionate about game development and eager to contribute to innovative projects, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity. Join our dynamic team and help us shape the future of indie games development!

Positions

    1. Junior UI/UX Designer:
    • Design the artistic elements and user interfaces for our game titles.
    • Reference No.: UI011
    • As a UI/UX Designer, you will be responsible for creating intuitive and engaging user interfaces for our game titles.
    • Salary: $50,000 - $70,000 per year
    • Reporting line: Lead UI/UX Director
    2. Junior Engine Programmer:
    • A position working on the development and maintenance of our game engine.
    • Reference No.: JP020
    • Writing performant, robust, and maintainable code, and debugging/profiling code.
    • Salary: $60,000 - $80,000 per year
    • Reporting line: Lead Engine Programmer
    3. DevOps Engineer:
    • Managing the infrastructure and deployment processes of our games.
    • Reference No.: DO130
    • Working closely with development teams to ensure applications are deployed efficiently.
    • Salary: $85,000 - $110,000 per year
    • Reporting line: Product Manager

Job Requirements

    Junior UI/UX Designer:
  • Proven experience as a UI/UX Designer or similar role
  • Portfolio demonstrating design skills and experience
  • Experience with Figma, Sketch, or Adobe Creative Suite
    • Bonus:
  • User-centered design principles knowledge
    Junior Engine Programmer:
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
  • Proficiency in C++ and C#
  • Experience with Unity or Unreal Engine
    • Bonus:
  • Experience with modern graphics APIs (Vulkan, D3D12)
    DevOps Engineer:
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
  • Experience with AWS, Azure, or GCP
  • Proficiency in automation tools and scripting
    • Bonus:
  • Understanding of Docker and Kubernetes

  6. Inclusive work environment
